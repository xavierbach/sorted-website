How it works

From newsletter to calendar
in three taps

No more manually copying dates. Sorted does the reading so you don't have to.

1

Import any way you like

Snap a photo, share a PDF from Files or Email, or use the built-in browser to import directly from a school website. Share straight from any app using the iOS share sheet.

2

AI reads it for you

Sorted scans the content and pulls out every event, date, and time — even from scanned pages, handwritten notices, or WhatsApp screenshots.

3

Review & save

Tap the events you want to keep and hit Save. They land straight in your calendar — Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook — with optional reminders. Add the lock screen widget for one-tap camera access next time.

Features

Everything a busy parent needs

AI-powered extraction

Uses advanced AI to read newsletters accurately, even when dates are written in natural language like "the last Friday of term".

Every format supported

Photos, PDFs, web pages, screenshots — share from any app or import directly. The original photo or PDF is saved inside Sorted and linked back to your calendar event so you can always find it again.

Profiles for every child

Create colour-coded profiles for each child and link their school, kinder, or care service. Events are tagged to the right child and land in the right calendar — perfect for families juggling multiple schools.

Smart reminders & widget

Set reminders days in advance — automatically added when you save. Add the lock screen widget for one-tap camera access, so you can capture a notice before you've even unlocked your phone.

Emoji event titles

Sorted adds a matching emoji to event titles so your calendar is instantly scannable — 🏏 Cricket, 🎂 Sam's 5th Birthday, 🔬 Science Excursion, and more.

Newsletter summaries Sorted+

After importing, Sorted+ members get an AI-generated summary — key dates, action items, and what matters, without reading the whole thing.

Private by design

Your data is never stored on our servers. Newsletter content is processed and immediately discarded. No accounts, no tracking.
