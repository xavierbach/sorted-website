How it works

From newsletter to calendar
in three taps

No more manually copying dates. Sorted does the reading so you don't have to.

1

Share your newsletter

Share a PDF or photo from your Files app, Camera, or Email. Works with any school newsletter format.

2

AI reads it for you

Sorted scans the newsletter and pulls out every event, date, and time — even from scanned pages.

3

Review & save

Features

Everything a busy parent needs

AI-powered extraction

Uses advanced AI to read newsletters accurately, even when dates are written in natural language like "the last Friday of term".

Photos & PDFs

Works with printed newsletters you've photographed, digital PDFs, and even scanned documents that are just images.

Multiple children

Set up profiles for each child and assign events directly to their calendar. Perfect for families at different schools.

Smart reminders

Choose to get reminders 1 day, 3 days, or a week before each event — automatically added when you save.

Emoji event titles

Sorted adds a matching emoji to event titles so your calendar is instantly scannable — 🏃 Sports Carnival, 🚌 Excursion, and more.

Private by design

Your data is never stored on our servers. Newsletter content is processed and immediately discarded. No accounts, no tracking.
